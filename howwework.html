How I Work

Strategy, relationships,

to yes.

The honest version

I don't offer packages.
I offer judgment.

I don't have a fixed methodology that I apply to every client regardless of where they are. What I offer is 25 years of experience building corporate partnership programs at organizations where the mission was everything — and the judgment to know what your specific situation actually needs.

Sometimes that's strategy from scratch — helping you figure out what you're selling and to whom. Sometimes it's full partnership development — finding prospects, building relationships, and closing deals. Sometimes it's a thought partner who shows up every few weeks and helps you think clearly.

We always start with a conversation to figure out what fits your moment.

01

Strategy & Positioning

Building the case for investment.

Before any outreach begins, I help you get clear on what you're offering and why a corporate partner should say yes. That means sharpening your value proposition, identifying the right types of partners to pursue, and building the assets that make the ask land.

This is the foundation that makes everything else more effective. Organizations that skip it often spend months pitching the wrong companies in the wrong way.

This might include

Partnership audit Value proposition development Partner prospect mapping Pitch deck development
02

Partner Development

Finding the right companies and getting them to yes.

I identify which companies to pursue, activate the relationships, and develop partnership proposals built around genuine shared value. Having spent years building corporate partnership programs from the inside, I understand what moves a company from interested to committed.

This isn't spray-and-pray. It's targeted, relationship-first work that takes time and benefits from both experience and the right collaborative team around it.

This might include

Prospect research & identification Relationship activation Partnership proposal development Negotiation support
03

Deal Structure & Close

Getting it across the line.

A great partnership conversation doesn't automatically become a signed deal. I help structure the agreement, navigate the negotiation, and ensure both sides feel good about what's been built — because the best partnerships are built to last, not just to close.

And I stay engaged through implementation to make sure what was promised actually gets delivered.

This might include

Deal structuring Contract negotiation support Implementation oversight Partnership activation
The Dancing Village model

It's not just me.
It's the right team for your project.

Dancing Village is a collaborative practice. I'm the constant — the strategist and relationship-builder at the center of every engagement. But I don't work alone, because the best work rarely happens that way.

Depending on what your project calls for, I bring in the right specialists: sector experts who know your field, researchers who can map the landscape, creatives who can sharpen your story, strategists who've navigated exactly your situation before.

The team is built around your needs — not a fixed roster that gets assigned regardless of fit.
